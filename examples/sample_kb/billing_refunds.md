# Billing and Refunds

The refund window is 30 days from the original purchase date. Refund requests submitted after 30 days are not eligible unless the product is defective.

To request a refund, contact support@example.com with your order number. Refunds are processed within 5–7 business days and returned to the original payment method.

Subscription cancellations take effect at the end of the current billing period. You will not be charged for the next period. Partial refunds for unused subscription time are not issued.

For billing disputes, submit a written request within 60 days of the charge date. Include your account ID, charge amount, and a brief explanation of the dispute.

Annual plan customers who cancel within the first 14 days receive a full refund. After 14 days, a pro-rated refund is issued for the unused portion of the year.
